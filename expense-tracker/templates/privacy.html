Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically:

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and restricted access to databases.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use a small number of trusted third-party services to help operate the app, such as hosting providers. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond providing their service to us.

We do not integrate with third-party advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics platforms that share data externally.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We will respond to all privacy-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.
